Eligibility Criteria

UG Programs (B.E./B.Tech)
  • Passed Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics
  • Minimum 60% aggregate in PCM (50% for SC/ST)
  • Valid TNEA / JEE Main score
  • Age: 17–23 years as of July 1, 2026
PG Programs (M.E./M.Tech)
  • B.E./B.Tech in relevant discipline
  • Minimum 60% aggregate
  • Valid TANCET / GATE score preferred
