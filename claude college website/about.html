About Prince College

Three decades of engineering excellence, innovation, and nation building

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution of excellence, producing technically competent, ethically strong, and socially responsible engineers who drive innovation and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Mission

  • Provide quality technical education aligned with industry needs
  • Foster a culture of research, innovation and entrepreneurship
  • Develop graduates with professional ethics and leadership skills
  • Strengthen industry-academia collaboration

Core Values

  • Academic Excellence
  • Integrity & Ethics
  • Innovation & Creativity
  • Inclusivity & Diversity
  • Social Responsibility
Our Journey

A Legacy of Excellence

1994

Founded

Prince College of Engineering established with 3 departments and 300 students under Anna University.

2000

NBA Accreditation

Received NBA accreditation for all UG programs, marking a milestone in academic quality.

2008

Campus Expansion

New 50-acre campus inaugurated with modern labs, sports complex and hostel facilities.

2015

NAAC A Grade

Awarded NAAC A Grade, recognizing excellence in academics, infrastructure and student outcomes.

2020

Research Centre

Established Centre of Excellence in AI & Machine Learning with ₹5 Crore funding.

2024

NAAC A+ Upgrade

Elevated to NAAC A+ status — among top 5% of engineering colleges in Tamil Nadu.
